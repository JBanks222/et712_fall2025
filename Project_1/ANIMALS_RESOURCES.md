# Animal Sounds and Images - Resources Guide

## Free Animal Sound Resources

### Recommended Sources:
1. **Mixkit** (https://mixkit.co/free-sound-effects/animals/)
   - Free animal sounds with no attribution required
   - High quality MP3 files
   - Search for: cat, dog, cow, sheep, duck, horse, lion, elephant, monkey, frog

2. **Freesound** (https://freesound.org)
   - Large collection of CC0 (free) animal sounds
   - Search for each animal individually
   - Check license - look for CC0 or attribution-free licenses

3. **SoundDino** (https://sounddino.com/en/effects/domestic-animals/)
   - Free domestic animal sounds
   - Good for farm animals

### File Organization:
Place all animal sound files in: `Project_1/audio/animals/`
- cat.mp3
- dog.mp3
- cow.mp3
- sheep.mp3
- duck.mp3
- horse.mp3
- lion.mp3
- elephant.mp3
- monkey.mp3
- frog.mp3

## Free Animal Image Resources

### Recommended Sources:
1. **Unsplash** (https://unsplash.com)
   - High-quality free photos
   - No attribution required
   - Search for each animal

2. **Pixabay** (https://pixabay.com)
   - Free images and illustrations
   - PNG format available
   - No attribution required for most images

3. **Pexels** (https://pexels.com)
   - Free stock photos
   - Good selection of animal photos

### File Organization:
Create the directory: `Project_1/assets/animals/`
Place animal images as PNG files:
- cat.png
- dog.png
- cow.png
- sheep.png
- duck.png
- horse.png
- lion.png
- elephant.png
- monkey.png
- frog.png

### Image Tips:
- Use transparent backgrounds (PNG format)
- Recommended size: 200x200px to 400x400px
- Look for cartoon/illustration style if you want a consistent look
- Search terms: "cartoon [animal]", "illustration [animal]", "[animal] clipart"

## Quick Download Steps:

1. **For Sounds:**
   - Visit Mixkit or Freesound
   - Search for each animal
   - Download as MP3
   - Save to `Project_1/audio/animals/` with lowercase filename

2. **For Images:**
   - Visit Pixabay or Unsplash
   - Search for each animal (try "cartoon" or "illustration" variants)
   - Download as PNG
   - Resize if needed (200-400px square)
   - Save to `Project_1/assets/animals/` with lowercase filename

3. **Test the Page:**
   - Open `animals.html` in a browser
   - Click randomize to see if images load
   - Click cards to test sounds
